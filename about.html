Scando History

In 1964, Bengt Magnusson opened Scandinavian Ski Shop with the goal of providing the people of Chicagoland with top-of-the-line ski equipment, along with expert service. Whether downhill or cross country, he always made sure the customer was leaving his store with the right products to serve their needs. Soon after, he helped organize the Trolls Ski Club for kids, which has given thousands of kids a chance to go skiing and enjoy the winter.
